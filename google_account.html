How to Create a Google Account

So you want to figure out how to make a Google account? It's actually really simple. The creation website is quite straightforward, but let me be the one to teach you!

Required Steps:

  1. Click here to go to the account creation page.
  2. Fill out the boxes that show up.

    It should ask you for:
    -Your First and Last name
    -An email with the name you choose
    -A password that you can make up
    Afer this, you will be taken to a follow up page.
  3. Next, it will ask you for:
    -Your phone number (optional)
    -A backup email (optional)
    -Your birthday
    -Your preferred gender

    Remember: Your phone number and backup email are only optional, you don't need them to create your accout.
  4. Finally, you should be taken to a Terms & Services page. Read it if you want, but it really doesn't even matter at this point because Google already has your personal info somehow.


    5. Press "I Agree" and there you go! You've now made your very own Google account. Nice job!
